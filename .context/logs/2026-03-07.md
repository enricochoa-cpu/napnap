### Glass Background Opacity Fix

- **Problem**: Cards in morning/afternoon themes looked like solid white blocks (stark contrast against warm cream page background).
- **Fix**: Reduced `--glass-bg` from `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.85)` to `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.55)` in both morning and afternoon themes.
- **Result**: Cards now feel integrated with the page, with warm background showing through the glass surface.
- Nav bar unchanged at `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.92)` for legibility.

### Save Button Reorder

- Moved Save button in BabyDetailView from after Measures/Share rows to before them.
- New order: Profile form → Gender → **Save** → Measures → Share → Delete.

## Decisions

1. **Report section deferred**: User not comfortable with current report screen. Commented out entirely (StatsView "Generate report" button + SleepReportView reference). Will be redesigned in a future session.
2. **Glass-bg 0.55 opacity**: Lower than typical glass values, but the warm cream backgrounds make this work. Cards feel part of the page rather than floating white rectangles.
3. **Save button before navigation rows**: Logically correct — save your profile changes before navigating to sub-views (Measures, Share).
