C: Drag-up to expand (Napper-style)

Sheet starts compact (times + buttons only). User drags up to reveal chips, pauses, and notes below. Closest to Napper's approach. But conflicts with existing drag-to-dismiss gesture.

Recommendation

Option A is the safest and simplest. The sheet stays compact by default, summary pills give a quick glance at what's been set, and tapping "Details" expands the full section. No gesture conflicts, works on all screen sizes. Option C is closest to Napper but introduces a drag-up gesture that fights with drag-to-dismiss.
