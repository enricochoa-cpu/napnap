Deep content page with at-a-glance card, prose sections, sample schedule, tips, and CTA. Best for SEO (long dwell time, comprehensive content). ~800-1200 words per page.

C. Hybrid (stats + article)

Stats strip at top for scanners, sample schedule, then 2-3 short prose sections for SEO depth. Best of both: quick reference for returning visitors + enough content for Google. ~500-700 words per page. My recommendation.
